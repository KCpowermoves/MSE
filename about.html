Our Story

Built to Serve Maryland Businesses

Maryland Smart Energy was founded with a single mission: make Maryland's state-funded energy efficiency programs accessible to every commercial business in the state. We saw that too many businesses were paying for energy inefficiency they did not have to — simply because navigating the utility rebate process was complex and time-consuming.

We became authorized service providers under the EmPOWER Maryland program for all four major Maryland utilities — BGE, Pepco, Delmarva Power, and SMECO. This lets us serve businesses across the entire state, handling every aspect of the program from eligibility confirmation through utility rebate submission.

Our approach is simple: we do the work, handle the paperwork, and you save money. There is no cost to you and no obligation beyond the initial conversation. The EmPOWER Maryland program is already funded through your utility bills — you are entitled to these benefits.
