# Maryland Smart Energy — Brand Guide

## Logo
- Icon: Maryland state seal / crab motif
- Wordmark: "Maryland Smart Energy"
- Tagline: "Smarter Buildings. Lower Bills."
- Usage: Always include tagline beneath wordmark. Never stretch or recolor.

## Colors

### Primary
- **Maryland Red:** `#C8102E` — buttons, icons, accents, CTA backgrounds
- **Gold/Amber:** `#F4A11D` — announcement bars, highlighted text, emphasis

### Neutrals
- **Dark Navy:** `#1A2332` — footer, dark section backgrounds, CTA blocks
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` — primary page background
- **Light Gray:** `#F5F5F5` — card backgrounds, secondary sections

### Never Use
- Default Tailwind blue or indigo
- Generic purple gradients
- Any color not listed above without explicit approval

## Typography

### Headings
- Font: Inter Bold or Barlow SemiBold
- Style: Dark navy base, red or gold used for emphasis words only
- Example: "Why Choose **Maryland Smart Energy**?" — company name in gold
- Tracking: Normal to slightly tight on large headings

### Body
- Font: Inter Regular or Source Sans Pro
- Size: 16px base, 1.6 line height
- Color: Dark gray `#333333`

### Buttons
- Primary: Red background `#C8102E`, white text, rounded corners
- Secondary: White background, dark border, dark text
- Hover: Darken primary by 10%

## Tone & Voice
- Professional and trustworthy — government-adjacent credibility
- Direct and clear — no jargon, no hype
- Lead with the "$0 cost" message every time
- Reassuring, not salesy
- Target audience: Maryland business owners and property managers

## Visual Style
- Clean, light, professional B2B aesthetic
- White backgrounds dominant
- Red and gold used as accents only — not overwhelming
- Icons: Simple line icons in red
- Photography: Real tradespeople, HVAC equipment, commercial buildings
- No stock photo cheese — authentic job-site imagery preferred
