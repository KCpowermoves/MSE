Delmarva EmPOWER Programs

What Delmarva Pays For in 2024–2026

Delmarva Power serves Maryland's Eastern Shore and participates in EmPOWER Maryland with competitive commercial rebates. Under the current program cycle, Delmarva small commercial customers can receive up to 80% of eligible project costs, with 50% available for medium and large businesses.

A standout feature of Delmarva's commercial programs is the Small Business Energy Advantage (SBEA) — a 0% interest financing program that allows qualifying businesses to finance energy upgrades over 24 months with no interest charges. This makes larger projects accessible even when upfront capital is limited.

Maryland Smart Energy is an authorized Delmarva Power service provider. We handle all program applications, work scheduling, and rebate tracking for Eastern Shore businesses.

Program Details

Delmarva Commercial Programs Explained

HVAC Tune-Up

Up to 80% Covered

Comprehensive commercial HVAC optimization for Eastern Shore businesses. Covers rooftop units, split systems, and package equipment that is at least 2 years old.

  • Refrigerant level verification and adjustment
  • Evaporator and condenser coil cleaning
  • Controls and thermostat calibration
  • Electrical connection inspection
  • Written efficiency assessment report

Building Tune-Up

Up to 80% (Small) / 50% (Large)

Whole-building energy optimization covering HVAC, lighting, building controls, and envelope. Delmarva offers small and large building programs for commercial properties up to 200,000 sqft.

  • Small buildings: under 75,000 sqft
  • Large buildings: 75,000–200,000 sqft
  • Monitoring-based commissioning option
  • HVAC and BAS controls optimization
  • Post-project verification included

Small Business Energy Advantage (SBEA)

0% Financing — 24 Months

Delmarva Power's unique financing program allows qualifying small businesses to finance energy upgrade projects over 24 months at 0% interest — repaid through bill installments. Stack SBEA financing on top of rebates to cover nearly the entire cost of a project.

  • 0% interest for 24 months
  • Repaid through monthly electric bill
  • Can be combined with rebate programs
  • No separate loan application or credit check
  • Ideal for HVAC replacements and lighting

Lighting Upgrades

Up to 80% Covered

LED lighting retrofits and controls upgrades for Eastern Shore commercial buildings. Delmarva offers both custom rebates (calculated per kWh saved) and instant discounts at participating distributors.

  • Interior and exterior LED fixtures
  • Lighting controls and occupancy sensors
  • Instant discounts at distributors
  • 90% energy reduction vs legacy HID/fluorescent
  • 10–15 year LED lifespan reduces maintenance
