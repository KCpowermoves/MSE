Pepco EmPOWER Programs

What Pepco Pays For in 2024–2026

Potomac Electric Power Company (Pepco) participates in EmPOWER Maryland and provides strong commercial rebates for businesses in Montgomery and Prince George's counties. Under the current cycle, Pepco small commercial customers can receive up to 80% of eligible project costs.

Pepco's commercial programs include HVAC tune-ups, building optimization, LED lighting, and an O&M training program that is 100% funded for qualifying businesses. Pepco also offers Building Instant Discounts of up to $263/ton on qualifying HVAC equipment replacements.

Maryland Smart Energy is an authorized Pepco service provider and handles all program applications, scheduling, and rebate tracking on your behalf.

Pepco O&M Training — 100% Covered

Pepco offers a unique Operations & Maintenance Training program that is 100% covered by EmPOWER Maryland. This program trains your facilities staff to optimize building energy performance — at zero cost to your business.

Program Details

Pepco Commercial Programs Explained

HVAC Tune-Up

Up to 80% Covered

Pepco's most utilized commercial rebate program. A comprehensive tune-up of your commercial HVAC systems reduces energy consumption 5–20% with no capital investment required.

  • Rooftop units, split systems, package equipment
  • Refrigerant level verification
  • Coil cleaning and inspection
  • Thermostat and controls calibration
  • Filter inspection and replacement

Building Tune-Up

Up to 80% (Small) / 50% (Large)

A whole-building energy optimization approach that targets HVAC controls, lighting, domestic hot water, and plug loads. Pepco offers both small building and large building programs with monitoring-based commissioning for large buildings.

  • Small buildings: under 75,000 sqft
  • Large buildings: 75,000–200,000 sqft
  • Monitoring-based commissioning option
  • HVAC and controls optimization included
  • Written savings report with recommendations

O&M Training (100% Covered)

Fully Funded by Pepco

A distinctive Pepco program: professional Operations & Maintenance training for your facilities staff. Pepco fully funds this program — your team learns energy-efficient building operation with no out-of-pocket cost.

  • Trained by certified energy professionals
  • HVAC operations and best practices
  • Lighting and controls management
  • Scheduling and setback programming
  • Preventive maintenance guidance

Building Instant Discounts

$263/ton on HVAC Equipment

Pepco offers instant point-of-purchase discounts on qualifying HVAC equipment through participating distributors. The $263/ton discount applies to eligible commercial HVAC systems — reducing the purchase price immediately rather than via rebate application.

  • Applied at distributor purchase
  • $263 per ton of cooling capacity
  • Qualifying ENERGY STAR equipment
  • No separate rebate application required
  • Compatible with separate tune-up rebates
