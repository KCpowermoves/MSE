Program Overview

What Is the Commercial HVAC Tune-Up Program?

The Commercial HVAC Tune-Up program is an EmPOWER Maryland initiative that allows Maryland businesses to receive a full professional tune-up of their HVAC systems at $0 after rebates. The program is funded through your utility's EmPOWER Maryland surcharge — meaning you have already been contributing to this program every month on your electric bill.

Maryland Smart Energy is an authorized service provider for this program under BGE, Pepco, Delmarva Power, and SMECO. We perform the technical work, handle all utility rebate applications, and submit all documentation on your behalf. You simply schedule a time and we do the rest.

Commercial HVAC systems that are not regularly maintained lose 5–10% efficiency per year. A professional tune-up restores peak performance, reduces energy consumption, and extends equipment life — all at zero cost to your business.

Scope of Work

What's Included in the Tune-Up?

Our certified HVAC technicians perform a thorough multi-point inspection, cleaning, and optimization of your commercial systems. Here is exactly what is covered:

Filter Replacement & InspectionReplace air filters and inspect filtration systems for proper airflow
Coil CleaningClean evaporator and condenser coils to restore heat exchange efficiency
Refrigerant Level CheckVerify refrigerant charge and check for leaks that reduce efficiency
Electrical ConnectionsInspect and tighten all electrical connections; test capacitors and contactors
Thermostat CalibrationVerify thermostat accuracy and calibrate for optimal temperature control
Drain Line CleaningClean condensate drain lines to prevent moisture buildup and mold
Belt & Motor InspectionInspect belts, pulleys, and motors; lubricate moving parts as needed
System Performance ReportFull written report of findings, efficiency rating, and recommendations
Eligibility

Does Your Business Qualify?

Most Maryland commercial businesses qualify for this program. Here are the basic requirements:

  • Active commercial electric account with BGE, Pepco, Delmarva Power, or SMECO
  • HVAC equipment at least 2 years old and in working condition
  • Located within the utility's Maryland service territory
  • Rooftop units, split systems, heat pumps, packaged units all qualify
  • No minimum or maximum building square footage requirement
  • Eligible even if you use a third-party electricity supplier

Not sure if you qualify? Call us at (301) 888-7090 and we will confirm your eligibility in minutes — at no obligation.
