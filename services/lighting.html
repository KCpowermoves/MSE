Program Overview

Why Upgrade Your Commercial Lighting?

Lighting typically represents 20–40% of a commercial building's total electricity consumption. Older fluorescent T8 and T12 tubes, HID high-bay fixtures, and incandescent lamps are dramatically less efficient than modern LED alternatives. A lighting upgrade is often the single fastest-payback energy improvement available to a commercial property.

Through EmPOWER Maryland, your utility will cover 75–80% of the project cost through rebates. Additionally, instant point-of-purchase discounts are available at participating distributors — no application required. Maryland Smart Energy handles the rebate application and guides you through both pathways.
